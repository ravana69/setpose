6 Sitting Poses to Use as a Reference for Drawing

October 19, 2021 - By August van de Ven

While dynamic poses are always fun to draw, drawing basic poses are a great way to challenge yourself while getting more familiar with the way the human body moves. Sitting poses, for example, probably seem quite simple and boring at first but when you try to draw them, you'll find that they can get quite challenging to draw.
